Welcome to Bibliotheca!

Bibliotheca (Latin for Library) is an online library catalog system designed to keep track of personal book collections.

You can create and share libraries of books with other users; add books manually, enter a ISBN number to have the data automatically enter for you, or scan the barcode on a book!

Bibliotheca also features the ability to search for books in your library by a number of options, as well as track when you loan books to others.
